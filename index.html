Introduction

Who are we?

The Mercenary Motorsports is a team of 25 mechanical engineering students from Bundelkhand Institute of Engineering & Technology, Jhansi who are passionate about automobiles. We design, analyse and manufacture Go-Karts to compete in domestic events and show off our engineering skills.
The team is equally divided into various departments such as Design & Analysis, Vehicle Dynamics, Engine & Transmission, Manufacturing, Sponsorship and Marketing.

About the Project

Team Mercenary Motorsports from BIET JHANSI is designing and building a formula style race car to compete in domestic and international events organized by Society of Automotive Engineers (SAE).
SAE Supra is India’s biggest engineering showdown in which student design and fabricate the open wheel Formula Type customized vehicle and compete with other teams in each and every aspect right from the static part to the dynamic. More than 150 teams show their metal at the competition.

Why are we doing this?

Every engineer dreams of using his theoretical knowledge on real world engineering applications. Competing in these events help us bring to life something special, built with our own hands. The sense of satisfaction we get when we see our ideas being implemented practically and working successfully is incomparable to any other feeling in the world.

How we do?

The whole process starts with CAD model of our vehicle followed by analysis, simulations and consultations. After virtual design is approved, the workshop becomes our home and sleepless nights become our life to convert our design into reality.
Once the vehicle comes alive, we rigorously test the vehicle for more than 200 kilometres to eliminate all sorts of design and manufacturing errors.

Our Achievements

Starting out as an underdog in 2016, we fabricated our first vehicle named “Protoko 1.0” and raced it at “Ultimate Karting Championship”, Bhopal. We bagged “The Best Design Award” and 12,000 cash prize but could not find a place on podium.

But to quench our thirst we analysed our shortcomings and made our vehicle faster, lighter as well as more reliable and upgraded it to “Protoko 2.0”. This time we competed in India’s biggest karting Festival “Indian Kart Racing” organised by ISIE, India held at India’s only F-1 circuit “Buddha International Circuit”, Greater Noida in Jan 2018. Our Go-kart completely dominated the event winning two dynamics awards and All India 2nd rank with 55,000 cash prize in total

The heat in the competition is very high so we have to brace ourselves to match and exceed other’s level. We are expanding our team and passing on the legacy to first and second years’. We are sweating it out day and night engulfing the theory books as well as engineering softwares to do things as we have planned.

Our Scope

How are we going to spend this money?

Why crowdfunding?

“Alone we go faster together we soar higher” Crowdfunding is the most transparent platform for reaching out to greater masses and to support individuals like us.

It takes a lot of time, effort and money to build an award-winning race car. It took almost 2200 man-hours to build our last car. All our free time and vacations are spent in the lab – building, testing and improving our car. For us work is worship and the car is our life. We literally “Live to Race”. Although, in a short span of time we have become one of the competing team in India, this doesn’t satisfy our hunger; we dream to become the best. We believe in ourselves and we need you to believe in us so that we can realize our dream.

What happens when I don't raise enough money?

If we are not able to collect enough money, the team will have to manufacture the next car on a scaled down budget, which will not be sufficient to build an outstanding car. There will be no point in building a car which doesn’t satisfy our standards and helps in continuing our progress.

What are the risks and challenges associated with our project?

Being a student team, the biggest challenge we face is the significant financial resources required for this project. We constantly need to innovate and think outside the box in order to improve our car. Our success depends on the successful implementation of these ideas, which is a difficult job. The risks involved in this project are minimal, as we have shown in the past that no challenge can stand between our dream and us.

Why should you believe me and why should you fund this project?

Winning is not just about achieving podium finish with gold, real victory lies in earning trust and making everyone a part of the celebration. The amount of trust you put in us, is of paramount importance.

Our team has been working tirelessly in order to build the best race car. The last thing we would want to do is tarnish our reputation and sacrifice our dream. Your contribution will be only be used to take our team to new heights..
